The Best Movies According to Aafreen

My favourite movies of all time.

The Pursuit of Happiness

This is a very nice movie.
It is about a hard working man who loves his family and comes across lots of challenges in life and succeeds in the end.

Life of Pi

This movie is about a man who gets stranded on a boat with a tiger.
The story is emotional and engages the viewer.

3 Idiots

This is the story of three college friends and how they go on to lead different lives only to realise that one of their friends was not who they thought he was.
